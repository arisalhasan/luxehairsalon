Our Story

Effortless, personal, and crafted with care. We opened Luxe Salon to make hair simpler and more joyful beautiful looks that fit your life, with a team that listens first.

From precision cuts and lived‑in color to extensions and smoothing, our stylists blend technique with an easygoing approach. You’ll leave with hair that looks great in the salon and is simple to style at home.

We believe great hair starts with a great experience: a calm space, real conversation, and a plan that puts your hair’s health first.
